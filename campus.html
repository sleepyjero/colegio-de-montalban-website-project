This works to keep the Colegio De Montalban community healthy and safe. It provides medical care, first aid, and advice to help both students and staff with their health needs and emergencies.

It serves as a central point for students, staff, and visitors to receive assistance and guidance. It's a valuable resource for navigating the school's facilities, accessing information, and finding support.

Canteen

It's a vital part of the school environment, serving as a hub for students to grab a quick bite, socialize, and recharge during breaks. It's a place where they can enjoy meals, snacks, and beverages in a designated space within the school grounds.

Covered Court

An open area used for sports, physical activities, events, and recreational purposes, providing space for students to play and exercise.
